Function (Biomass): f(x) = L / (1 + e-k(x-x₀))
Derivative (Growth Rate): f'(x) = k · f(x) · (1 - f(x)/L)

Parameters based on Malaysian Palm Oil Board (MPOB) growth yield curves.
